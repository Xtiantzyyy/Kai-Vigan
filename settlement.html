VIGAN CITY HERITAGE

Located in Vigan, 600 m from Calle Crisologo, Paradores de Vigan provides accommodation with a fitness centre, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

EA APARTELLE

Located in Vigan, 4.1 km from Calle Crisologo, EA Apartelle - Metro Vigan provides accommodation with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. This 2-star hostel offers free WiFi. The accommodation offers airport transfers, while a car rental service is also available.

THE RIVER HOUSE

Situated in San Vicente, The River House - Loft Units features air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony and free WiFi. This holiday home has a garden and free private parking. This holiday home includes 4 units, a living room and a flat-screen TV, a coffee corner with dining area, 1 bathroom with hoth/cold shower.

HOTEL VENETO DE VIGAN

The century-old mansion turned into a museum is owned by Floro S. Crisologo. He was the patriarch of the clan and a congressman known for being responsible for landmark legislations that not only benefited his constituents but the whole country as well.The tragic event in his life was the reason for opening the museum.

ESCOLTA'S HOMEY LODGE

Situated in Vigan, 200 m from Calle Crisologo, Escolta's Homey Lodge features accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking and a garden. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and a shared kitchen, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The hostel has family rooms.

EL SENOR JESUS APARTELLE

Set within 1.9 km of Calle Crisologo and 2.4 km of Bantay Bell Tower, RedDoorz El Senor Jesus Apartelle Vigan Ilocos offers rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Vigan.
